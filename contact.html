Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a product or brand for review?

We love hearing about new discoveries! Send us an email with details about the product or brand, including why you think it aligns with our vintage and quality-focused values. We review all suggestions and prioritize those that fit our mission of promoting timeless, sustainable choices.

Do you accept guest posts or collaborations?

Yes! We're always interested in working with like-minded creators who share our passion for vintage living and sustainable choices. Please send us your proposal including your background, content ideas, and examples of your work.

How do you choose which products to recommend?

Our recommendations are based on extensive research, personal testing, and alignment with our values of quality, authenticity, and sustainability. We prioritize products with heritage, craftsmanship, and timeless appeal. We never recommend products we wouldn't use ourselves.

Can I request content on specific topics?

Absolutely! We value our readers' interests and often create content based on requests. Use our contact form to suggest topics you'd like us to explore, and we'll do our best to address them in future articles.

Do you offer consulting services?

We offer limited consulting services for businesses and individuals looking to incorporate vintage elements into their lifestyle or brand. Contact us with details about your project, and we'll let you know if it's something we can help with.
